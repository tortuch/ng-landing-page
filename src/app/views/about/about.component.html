About

What is pannotation?

PanNotation is an online subscription-based portal that hosts a wealth of information about the steelpan and it facilitates the purchasing and downloading of music scores. Users can upload music scores or any other materials to our site. Composers and arrangers will earn per download of music scores. This site was created to fulfill a need within the steelpan community. Pannists around the world can now have a single location to upload and download music scores.

HOW CAN I UPLOAD MY MUSIC SCORE TO THE SITE FOR PURCHASE?

Users can upload music scores via an email sent to our team. Our team attains permission from the composer and arranger (if the user does not function as one of these roles) with assistance from the Copyright Organization of Trinidad and Tobago (COTT). Once approvals are given, the user will be given pricing guidelines, a price is decided, and the score is then made available on the site. Once another user purchases the score, the cost of the music score is divided between the composer, arranger, and PanNotation. Percentages will be made clear to the user before upload is complete. If the user is the composer and there is no arranger, then the cost of the music score is divided between the composer and PanNotation.

WHAT MATERIALS DO SUBSCRIBERS HAVE ACCESS TO?

  • Music Scores
  • Lead Sheets
  • Academic Publications
  • Audio Recordings
  • Steelband Data from Around the World
  • Historical Data
  • Podcasts

Subscribers can upload any of these materials to the site via email. Our team conducts a few quality checks and then makes the material available for subscribers on the site.

WHY USE PANNOTATION?

CURRENT SITUATION

  • Access to pan scores are limited
  • Limited local publishing
  • Arrangers aren’t able to legally sell arrangements of internationally popular music
  • Little advertising for local pan businesses
  • Limited Not-for-Profits

PANNOTATION’S SOLUTION

  • Access pan scores worldwide
  • Local composers have a platform
  • Arrangers can now sell arrangements of any genre because PanNotation will work on permissions on behalf of the user.
  • One location for academic publications
  • Advertising for local businesses available
  • PanNotation’s Not-for-Profit

PANNOTATION’S NOT-FOR-PROFIT

All proceeds from the PanNotation site will be dedicated to the Not-for-Profit arm of PanNotation. By subscribing to PanNotation, you will be contributing to the funding of steelpan scholarships, grants, transcriptions services, financial assistance with international performances, and the funding of other steelpan projects. PanNotation not only aims to create a platform for composers, arragners, educators, and pannists to share and earn from hard work rendered, but we also aim to give back to our communities and assist in creating new opportunities for pannists around the world.
